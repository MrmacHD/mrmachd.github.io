After a while of having "no experiments available yet." in the experiments section of the site, I have updated it with several of my open source projects.

They are written in many programming languages, some of which work across multiple platforms like those written in Java or Python. Others are OS X specific, like those written in Objective-C. If one of them depends on some library or framework, those are mentioned in its prerequisites sub-section.

Hope you enjoy the new experiments section, as it offers something for everybody; plus it's free. I will update it with more of my open source projects, as they are being developed. Finally, if any of those experiments has been of great value to you, I happily accept donations too.
