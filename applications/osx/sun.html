Sun

Sun is an OS X utility application. It is plain and simple a secondary calendar besides the one under OS X Clock menu. People in different parts of the world use different calendars. Some people even need two or more calendars in order to work on a daily basis. Sun should help anyone who needs that secondary calendar right besides the OS X default calendar.

Tips & Tricks

  • under Settings one can force Sun to show the current date right in the menu bar, thereby avoiding that extra mouse click to reveal the date.
